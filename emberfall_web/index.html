Emberfall, a playable arcade game where you defend a forest from falling embers and spreading fire. Tap embers and beetles to swat them, tap burning trees to douse them, and choose a ground tool to plant free seedlings, plant fire-resistant climate-adapted trees using points, or dig fire lines that block fire from spreading between trees. Dead snags breed beetle outbreaks, black-backed woodpeckers arrive to eat the beetles, surviving trees fling cones to reseed burned ground, periodic rain suppresses fire, and the fire intensifies as time passes.
